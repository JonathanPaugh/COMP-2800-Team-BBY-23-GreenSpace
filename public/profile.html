Our company, Veggie Farms, has evolved from a small vegetable nursery into a world-class vegetable wholesaler with facilities in three states. We specialize in organic, grass-fed and hormone-free vegetables. We don't use any pesticides or synthetic fertilizers. Our organic vegetable garden is located in the heart of the Midwest. Our greenhouse is located in Ohio. Our meat, eggs, dairy, honey, fish and poultry are all grown in our Cincinnati location. The company is operated by the Schuster family and is led by CEO Frank Schuster.
